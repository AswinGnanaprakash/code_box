
CURE TREATMENT FOR CORN NORTHERN LEAF BLIGHT DISEASE
===========================================================

1. Crop rotation to reduce previous corn residues and disease inoculum.

2. Scouting fields and monitoring local conditions can control this disease.

3. Use tillage to improve harvestability on infected plants

4. Add Foliar fungicides to northern corn leaf blight which may reduce diseased area.
