
CURE TREATMENT FOR TOMATO LEAF MOLD DISAEASE
==================================================

1. Crop residue should be removed.

2. Sanitize greenhouse to prevent it from infection.

3. Minimize long periods of leaf wetness by avoiding wetting the foliage and the use of fans to circulate air for curing.

4. Staking and pruning to increase ventilation reduces disease spread.

5. Fungicides effectively cures this disease.
