
CURE TREATMENT FOR CORN COMMON RUST DISEASE
==================================================

1. Watering in the early morning hours with drip irrigation can reduce the infection of plants.

2. Destory and burn infected leaves but do not compost. 

3. Prune , stake and use organic fertilizer on infected crop.

4. Use copper sprays or sulfur powders to prevent infection of susceptible corn plants.

5. Apply as a protective spray (2.5 oz/ gallon) to the infected plant.
