
CURE TREATMENT FOR GRAPE ESCA DISEASE
==========================================

1. Lime sulfur sprays helps to cure.

2. pruning cuts are done and infected parts are removed out.

3. Ashed pruning shears and grafting machines are used on infected plants.

4. Chemical dips and sprays used for the control of external pathogens .

5. Chinosol (Hydroxyquinoline sulfate) was reported to be the most commonly used fungicide for cure.
