
CURE TREATMENT FOR TOMATO TARGET SPOT DISEASE
====================================================

1. Minimize long periods of leaf wetness by avoiding wetting the foliage and the use of fans to circulate air for curing.

2. Staking and pruning to increase ventilation reduces disease spread.

3. Fungicides effectively cures this disease.

4. Removing infected leaves from the plants.

5. Mulch infected parts of the plants.
