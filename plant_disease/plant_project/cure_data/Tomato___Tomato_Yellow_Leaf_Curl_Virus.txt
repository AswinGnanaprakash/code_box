
CURE TREATMENT FOR TOMATO YELLOW LEAF CURL VIRUS DISEASE
================================================================

1. Water tomato plants carefully at base to keep it dry for cure.
 
2. Watering early in the day is best to reduce infection.

3. Remove all the infected plants and destroy them.

4. Cover the aluminum foil mulches to cure the infected plants.

5. Apply V Bind  viricides for the diseased parts of plant.
