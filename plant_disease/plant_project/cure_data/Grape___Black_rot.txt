
CURE TREATMENT FOR GRAPE BLACK ROT DISEASE
================================================
1. Pruning and training is done for curing.

2. Cut off the obviously affected parts of the grape vine with a sterile knife.

3. Place fans in the growing area to keep the plants dry this helps spreading of rots disease.

4. Apply a paste of cinnamon mixed with cooking oil to the remaining tissues of the plant that helps to cure.

5. Apply the fungicide paste to the new cuts until the black rot has been cured.
