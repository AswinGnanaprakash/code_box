
CURE TREATMENT FOR POTATO EARLY BLIGHT DISEASE
====================================================

1. Prune or stake plants to improve air circulation and reduce fungal problems.

2. Cut the infected parts and spary one part bleach to 4 parts water to it.

3. Add a layer of organic compost to prevent the spores in curing.

4. Drip irrigation is used for managment.

5. Burn infected plant parts and apply copper-based fungicides spray for curing.
