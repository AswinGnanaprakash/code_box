
CURE TREATMENT FOR ORANGE HAUNGLONGBING DISEASE
=======================================================

1. Amounts of nutrients are increased to infected leaves of plants.

2. Reflective metalized polyethylene is used as mulch for curing citrus plants.

3. Heating Haunglongbing infected parts can potentially prolong the productivity of plants.

4. Applying insecticides can remove infected orange citrus plants.

5. Planting and grafting HLB citrus plants can also control the diseased parts.
