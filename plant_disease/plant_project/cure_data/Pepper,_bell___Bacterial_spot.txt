
CURE TREATMENT FOR PEPPER BELL BACTERICAL SPOT DISEASE
============================================================

1. Use a thick layer of mulch to cover the soil, Mulch will reduce weeds and prevent from disease.

2. Prune or stake plants to reduce plant infection. 

3. Spraying with a baking soda is solution fir cure.

4. Apply sulfur sprays or copper-based fungicides to cure and avoid spreading of diseases.

5. Infected plants are treated with SERENADE Garden organic fungicided.
