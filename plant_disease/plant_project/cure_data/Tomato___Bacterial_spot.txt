
CURE TREATMENT FOR TOMATO BACTERIAL SPOT DISEASE
======================================================

1. Hot water treatment can be used to kill bacteria on tomato plants.

2. Sprays with copper or streptomycin acts as an active ingredient which is effective in preventing bacterial diseases.

3. Pesticides are also available to protect tomatoes from bacterial spot.

4. Minimizing leaf wetness can control diseased plants.

5. Sanitize effectively by solarization or washing with disinfectant for cure treatment.
