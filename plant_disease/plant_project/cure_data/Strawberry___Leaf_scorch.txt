
CURE TREATMENT FOR STRAWBERRY LEAF SCORCH DISEASE
========================================================

1. Use organic fungicide like Aliette WDG for treatment.

2. The best way to treat this disease is to alter watering practices and allow for dryer conditions. 

3. You can also treat your strawberries with organic fungicides like Captan 50 WP and Copper compounds for curing.

4. Removing infected leaves after harvest is helpful in reducing inoculum. 

5. Watering the leaves can control weeds and infected leaves in the planting.
