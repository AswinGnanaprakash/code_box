
CURE TREATMENT FOR PEACH BACTERIAL SPOT DISEASE
=====================================================

1. proper pruning should be done for infected plants.

2. Fertilize and water as necessary.

3. spray with copper based bactericide and the antibiotic oxytetracycline for curing.

4. Kondodys Bordaeaux Paint - Organic Bio Fungicide is used for infected plants.

5. Use dodine 65 WP plus Captan 50 WP (1/2 lb. plus 1 lb. per 100 gal. water) for disease control.
