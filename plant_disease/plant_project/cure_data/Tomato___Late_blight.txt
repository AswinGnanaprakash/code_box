
CURE TREATMENT FOR TOMATO LATE BLIGHT DISEASE
===================================================

1. The infected plants should be removed.

2. Then the infected plant is disced-under. 

3. They can be killed with herbicide or flame-killed to avoid spreading through the entire field.

4. Pesticides are also available to protect tomatoes from Late blight.

5. Use of SERENADE is beneficial for curing.
