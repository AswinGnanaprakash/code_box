
CURE TREATMENT FOR APPLE SCAB DISEASE
=========================================

1. Rake under trees and destroy infected leaves to reduce the number of fungal spores.

2. Spray the apple tree foliar 3-4tsp/gallon water with a fertilizer mixture to the diseased leaves.

3. Apply the urea/Bonide Sulfur Plant Fungicide to infected fallen leaves to decompose.

4. Run a lawn mower and use mulcher over the fallen leaves multiple time for cure treatment.

5. For best control, spray liquid copper soap.
