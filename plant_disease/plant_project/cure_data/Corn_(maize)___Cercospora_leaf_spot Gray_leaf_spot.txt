
CURE TREATMENT FOR CORN CERCOSPORA LEAF SPOT DISEASE
===========================================================

1. Prune or stake plants to improve air circulation of infected plants.

2. Apply sulfur sprays or copper-based fungicides weekly which kills gray leaf spots.

3. Use of mulch will reduce weeds and prevent the disease pathogens.

4. SERENADE Garden bio-fungicide chemaical fertilizers are used on infected parts.

5. Spraying water with a baking soda solution can cure the diseased plant.
