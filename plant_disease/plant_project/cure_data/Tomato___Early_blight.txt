
CURE TREATMENT FOR TOMATO EARLY BLIGHT DISEASE
====================================================

1. The infected branches or shoots should be pruned out several inches below visible signs of the infection.

2. The infected branches should be burned or buried.

3. Pesticides are also available to protect tomatoes from Early blight.

4. Cut the infected parts and spary one part bleach to 4 parts water to it.

5. Add a layer of organic compost to prevent the spores in curing.
