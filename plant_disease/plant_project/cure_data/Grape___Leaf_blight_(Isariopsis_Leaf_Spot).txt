
CURE TREATMENT FOR LEAF BLIGHT DISEASE
==========================================

1. Fungicides spray helps to reduce the disease.

2. Combine one part whole milk to nine parts water, mix well and transfer to a spray bottle for organic treatment.

3. Spray grapevines and infected leaves with copper-based fungicides .

4. Apply neem oil or potassium bicarbonate to control diseased leaves.

5. Cover the infected leaves with plastic sheeting prevents spreading of disease.
